UEH Boundary

Universal Exchange Adapters (UEH) is survivability-first infrastructure for operating against unstable exchange APIs. UEH does not provide trading signals, strategies, or execution services.

What UEH Is / Is Not

  • Infrastructure for exchange connectivity and operational boundaries
  • NO-EXEC by default (public artifacts are proof-only)
  • Not a trading bot, signal service, or profit-guarantee product

Non-Negotiable Boundaries

  • No custody of user funds
  • No profit or performance guarantees
  • No public execution modules
  • Clear responsibility separation (decision / execution / exchange)

Delivery Conditions

Commercial delivery begins only after explicit boundary acknowledgment and payment confirmation. Scope, constraints, and refusal rules are fixed before any delivery.

Public Proof (NO-EXEC)

Public demonstrations and documentation exist only in non-executing form. Commercial constraint packs and operational tooling are delivered privately.
